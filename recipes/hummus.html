Hummus

About the dish

This creamy hummus dish is inspired by middle eastern cousine.
It's mainly made by blended chickpeas and spices.

Works well as a dip, topping or side-dish with other food.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas 230g
  • Lemon juice 3Tbs
  • Olive oil 2Tbs
  • Salt 1ts
  • Garlic 1/2 clove
  • Cumin 2ts
